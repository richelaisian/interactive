Forum rules
Although BMXMUSEUM.com does not & cannot review the Messages posted & are not responsible for the content of any of these Messages, we do reserve the right to Delete any Thread/Post/Image or Restrict/Ban any Member at anytime for any or no reason whatsoever.

Multiple Accounts are not Tolerated, do not start more than one Account.

This is not an Appraisal Site. Please Do not ask "What's It Worth?"

Registration is required if you plan to post Topics or reply to existing Topics. You are not required to post your real name, but you are required to post your actual email address. Using fake emails or IPs will result in Ban/Deletion from the Site.

Basic rule of posting is USE COMMON SENSE, if you lack said Common Sense, don't post. Unsure? Read the Forums (and their posted rules), use the Search Function or enlist the help of a Moderator or Site Member before posting. Specific rules for each Forum can be found in a Topic at the top of the first page in each Section, please READ before posting.

USE THE SEARCH BUTTON! Duplicate Threads help no one so please make sure to Search out a Topic before starting one, your Question may already been Answered 10x over.

The "BMXmuseum.com Bikes" Section is NOT a For Sale/Trade/Want To Buy Section, we have a separate Classifieds Section for things of that nature. Bikes found in the "BMXmuseum.com Bikes" Section in Violation of this will be Deleted without Notification.

It is not permitted to post links to your For Sale/Trade/Want To Buy threads outside of those sections and a warning will be sent if you do.

Advertising anything on Site(Companies/other forums/etc.)needs to be discussed beforehand. Please email or Private Message the Site Owner, GAry, at gary@bmxmuseum.com

Any constantly problematic Member(Example: Someone signing up with 20+ accounts)will be reported to their Internet Service Provider for Violating their specific provider's Terms of Service as well as our Site Rules.

Threatening Bodily Harm towards any Members is not Tolerated. Trolling, disruptive or abusive behaviour will result in a warning, post restriction or banning. This Site is used by Members of all ages & the content should reflect that, no XXX pictures etc... No posting of guns, related items or weapons of any sort in avatars, signatures or posts. They are not needed here and will be deleted with a warning issued.
