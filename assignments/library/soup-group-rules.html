~SOUP GROUP~
rules:

1. 

posts must contain an original picture of soup* that has been consumed in the presence of the poster.

2. 

all other soup related content must be placed in the description or comments of a proper soup post.

3. 

have fun!

4. 

those are the rules, that's all of them, all the rules we got.

* 

no cereal allowed! cereal is not a soup!

* 

chili/pudding/other dubious liquidy foods: use the plate test! if you put it on a plate, would it pile or would it spread and fall off? if the answer is pile, it's not a soup!
